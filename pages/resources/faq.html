Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform, courses, and AI learning resources.

Courses

What types of AI courses do you offer?

NeuraLeap offers a wide range of AI courses, including beginner introductions to AI concepts, advanced deep learning, specialized courses on large language models (LLMs), neural networks, computer vision, natural language processing, AI ethics and safety, and practical AI application development. Our curriculum is designed to cater to learners at all levels, from those with no prior experience to advanced practitioners looking to specialize.

Do I need prior programming experience for your courses?

It depends on the course. Our beginner courses are designed to be accessible to those with no prior programming experience and will teach you the necessary programming skills alongside AI concepts. For intermediate and advanced courses, basic programming knowledge (particularly in Python) is recommended. Each course listing clearly indicates the prerequisites so you can choose the right starting point for your skill level.

How long does it take to complete a course?

Course duration varies depending on the complexity and depth of the material. Introductory courses typically take 4-6 weeks to complete when studying 5-7 hours per week. Intermediate courses range from 8-12 weeks, and our advanced specializations can take 12-16 weeks. However, all our courses are self-paced, so you can progress according to your own schedule and learning speed.

Platform

Can I access the course materials on mobile devices?

Yes, the NeuraLeap platform is fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices. You can access all course materials, videos, and readings on smartphones and tablets. However, for coding exercises and practical assignments, we recommend using a desktop or laptop computer for the best experience, as coding on mobile devices can be challenging.

Do you provide certificates upon course completion?

Yes, upon successful completion of a course (including all assignments and assessments), you will receive a digital certificate that you can share on your professional profiles like LinkedIn. Our certificates are recognized in the industry and can help demonstrate your AI skills to potential employers. For certain advanced courses, we also offer professional certifications that involve more rigorous assessment.

Account

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter the email address associated with your account, and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact our support team for assistance.

Can I change my email address or username?

Yes, you can change your email address and profile information from your account settings. After logging in, navigate to the "Profile" or "Account Settings" section, where you'll find options to update your personal information. Note that changing your email address will require verification of the new email to ensure account security.

Payment

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and bank transfers for course payments. For certain regions, we also support local payment methods. Corporate clients can request invoice-based payments for team enrollments. All payments are processed securely through our payment partners.

Do you offer refunds if I'm not satisfied with a course?

Yes, we offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for most of our courses. If you're not satisfied with the course content, you can request a full refund within 14 days of purchase, provided you haven't completed more than 25% of the course material. To request a refund, contact our support team with your order details. Some specialized or limited-time courses may have different refund policies, which will be clearly stated on the course page.

Are there any discounts or scholarships available?

We offer several discount programs, including early bird pricing for new courses, bundle discounts when purchasing multiple courses, and special promotions throughout the year. We also have a scholarship program for students, educators, and individuals from underrepresented groups in tech. To apply for a scholarship, visit our "Scholarships" page and complete the application form. Corporate clients can contact us for volume pricing when enrolling multiple team members.
